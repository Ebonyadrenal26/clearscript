# 📝 clearscript - Edit transcripts with your local intelligence

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Clearscript-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Ebonyadrenal26/clearscript/releases)

## 🧠 Setting Up Your Model

Clearscript requires an external artificial intelligence model to convert speech into text. This keeps the process private and local.

1. Open the application.
2. Navigate to the "Settings" tab in the main menu.
3. Find the "Model Path" field.
4. Point this field to the folder where you keep your preferred recognition model files.
5. If you do not have a model, you can download files compatible with this application from recognized AI research hubs. 
6. Save your settings. The application will verify the model once it loads.

## 📚 Building Your Terminology Library

The app includes a feature to store custom terms. This makes editing specific topics easier.

1. Click on the "Library" icon in the sidebar.
2. Select "Add Term."
3. Type the word you want the app to recognize.
4. Add a definition or a standard spelling if needed.
5. The application will use this list to suggest corrections while you review your transcripts.

## 🖋 Using the Editor

The editor keeps your transcription process simple.

1. Click "File" and then "Open Transcript."
2. Select your text file or audio file.
3. Read through the generated text in the main window.
4. Click on any word to fix typos or adjust punctuation.
5. The application highlights sections where it detected potential errors.
6. Press "Save" to keep your changes to your local drive.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the app connect to the internet?**
The app runs on your computer. It does not send your data to external servers. It only accesses the internet if you choose to sync your settings or download new updates.

**How do I update the software?**
When a new version releases, visit the download link provided at the top of this page. Download the new installer and run it. The new version will overwrite the old one while keeping your settings and terminology library intact.

**Can I use it with other tools?**
You can export your finished text into standard document formats. This lets you move your work into word processors or publishing software easily.

**What should I do if the app stops responding?**
Close the application fully using the Task Manager. Restart it. If the issue persists, check that your model files remain in the folder you specified in the settings menu.

## 🔏 Privacy and Security

Clearscript prioritizes your data. Since you manage your own model, you control which data processing instructions reach your files. No service provider tracks your usage or logs your transcription history. All files remain on your device's hard drive unless you move them through your file explorer.
